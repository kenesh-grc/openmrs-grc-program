## Overview
This document summarizes the current security, risk, and compliance posture of the OpenMRS healthcare system supporting electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI).

## Program Scope
- OpenMRS application
- AWS cloud infrastructure
- HIPAA Security & Privacy Rules
- Supporting governance and incident response processes

## Risk Posture Summary
The overall risk posture is **Moderate**. Identified risks are primarily related to access management, third-party dependencies, and cloud configuration.

## Key Risks
- Unauthorized access to ePHI
- Cloud misconfiguration exposure
- Third-party vendor handling of sensitive data

## Control Effectiveness
Security and administrative controls were tested and demonstrated a **100% pass rate**. No critical control deficiencies were identified.

## Incident Readiness
An incident response plan is in place and was validated through a tabletop exercise simulating an ePHI breach. Improvement actions were identified and tracked.

## Compliance Status
- HIPAA Security Rule: Substantially Compliant
- HIPAA Privacy Rule: Partially Compliant
- SOC 2 Readiness: In Progress

## Key Recommendations
- Enforce MFA for all privileged accounts
- Enhance third-party risk assessments
- Continue quarterly risk reviews

## Conclusion
The OpenMRS GRC program demonstrates a structured and effective approach to managing security and compliance risks in a healthcare environment.
